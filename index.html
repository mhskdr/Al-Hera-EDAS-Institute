Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Our kindergarten nurtures young minds with a blend of playful learning and Islamic values, fostering curiosity and a love for knowledge from the very beginning.

Elementary School

In our elementary section, students explore modern education intertwined with cultural heritage, developing essential skills and strong moral foundations to face the future confidently.

Middle (10-16) Years

Our middle school prepares students for the challenges ahead, combining rigorous academics with spiritual growth, leadership training, and critical thinking for well-rounded development.
